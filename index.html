Mental Health Crisis

Written by Miguel Taveras

Welcome to my blog, my name is Miguel and I am writing about the Mental Health Crisis in the United States of America. The pandemic of 2020 changed the world forever. Mental health is on the Rise in the United States. Men women and children of all ages have been affected in one way or another. If you know someone who is going through a hard time in life, offer your support, whether its getting them professional help or being there for them in these tough times.

According to an article by CNN about 1 in 5 adults received mental health services in the past year.

The Pandemic was rough on alot of people, but not everyone is seeking help. According to CNN some people dont have time or some are embarrassed to get the help they need.
